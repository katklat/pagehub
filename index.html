About Me

I'm someone who enjoys working at the intersection of data, engineering, and problem-solving. After receiving my M.Sc. in Biotechnology, I worked in pharmaceutical research, producing and analyzing data to support scientific decision making. In this context, I started programming to streamline my data pipelines.

Soon, I realized I was passionate about code and applications. So I moved on to build backends for web applications in the logistics and insurance sectors, where I learned best practices in software development.

My experiences in software development and data analytics sparked a shift toward data and analytics engineering, where I can blend my background in web development with a deep curiosity for how data flows, transforms, and creates value. I enjoy working with tools like Airflow, dbt and Spark. My preferred programming language is Python.

I am interested in sustainability, NLP, music theory, science and language learning among other things.
